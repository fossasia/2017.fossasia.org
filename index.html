Topics

Technology and Startups

Join us in March 2017 and learn about new developments in software and hardware. Meet developers of Open Source knitting machines, Laptops that can send and receive on all frequencies, web technologies like Javascript, PHP, Ruby, HTML, Git, Android, DevOps, Docker and much more.

OpenTech and Science Hacks

The FOSSASIA weekend in March 2017 is dedicated to Open Technologies. What are the technologies and scientific achievements that make our world run? We have tracks on Open Source software and customized hardware, Arduinos, Rapsberry PI, Open Design applications and the maker and hacker community. In the Science Hack tracks you will learn about how you can participate in the Citizen Science community.

A little more about FOSSASIA

FOSSASIA is the premier Free and Open Source technology event in Asia for developers, start-ups, and contributors. Projects at FOSSASIA range from open hardware, to design, graphics and software. FOSSASIA was established in 2009. Previous events took place in Cambodia and Vietnam.

FOSSASIA is set over three days of ground-breaking talks and workshops for OpenTech enthusiasts

FOSSASIA Tech Kids and Science Kids

At the FOSSASIA Science and Tech Kids track students learn to code their first mobile apps, build paper electronics (so called chibotronics), set up robots with a raspberry PI and make Science "hack" experiments. Kids workshops take place on Saturday and Sunday and start at 10am and 2pm.
